About this item
Adds natural tint to the lips: RENEE Lip Fix 3-in-1 Lip Balm is crafted with a unique dual core design. Its outer layer adds a natural flush of color to the lips with its smooth and moisturizing formula that contains Shea butter, Jojoba oil and Castor oil.
